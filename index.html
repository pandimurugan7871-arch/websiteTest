About Us

ExploreWorld is a premier travel agency that connects you with breathtaking destinations — from snow-capped peaks to tropical beaches. We offer custom tours, solo trips, family vacations, and luxury packages tailored to your dreams.


Our experienced team works around the clock to curate the best travel experiences suited for every kind of traveler. Whether you're looking to unwind, explore nature, experience culture, or indulge in adventure, we have the perfect journey waiting for you.


With ExploreWorld, every trip becomes a story worth telling. We believe travel is more than just sightseeing — it’s about creating memories, building relationships, and gaining new perspectives.


Our services include seamless booking experiences, knowledgeable local guides, flexible itineraries, and 24/7 support throughout your journey. We also partner with eco-friendly lodges and sustainable tourism initiatives to ensure that your travel has a positive impact on the communities you visit.

Packages

Choose from a variety of travel packages tailored to your taste and budget. Our most popular options include:

Himalayan Adventure Treks

Duration: 7 Days

Price: ₹25,000 per person

Experience guided mountain treks through the Himalayas, complete with evening campfires, rustic stays in remote villages, and breathtaking panoramic views in Himachal and Uttarakhand.

Romantic Goa Getaway

Duration: 4 Days

Price: ₹18,500 per couple

Stay at luxury beachfront resorts, enjoy thrilling water sports, candlelight beach dinners, and a guided nightlife tour through Goa’s vibrant party scene.

Kerala Backwater Tours

Duration: 5 Days

Price: ₹22,000 per person

Relax on a scenic boat house cruise through Kerala's backwaters, indulge in authentic cuisine, and unwind with traditional Ayurvedic massages and wellness treatments.

Luxury Rajasthan Heritage Trip

Duration: 6 Days

Price: ₹30,000 per person

Live like royalty in heritage palaces, ride camels through golden dunes, and witness mesmerizing cultural folk dances and music in Jaipur, Udaipur, and Jaisalmer.

Customized International Tours

Price: Varies by destination

Dreaming of exploring Bali’s beaches, shopping in Dubai, or enjoying theme parks in Singapore? Get a personalized itinerary with accommodation, transfers, and sightseeing designed just for you.

Andaman Island Escapes

Duration: 5 Days

Price: ₹28,000 per person

Discover the pristine beaches, coral reefs, and marine life of the Andaman Islands. This tour includes guided snorkeling, boat rides, and visits to Radhanagar Beach, Cellular Jail, and Havelock Island.

North East Explorer

Duration: 6 Days

Price: ₹24,500 per person

Explore the mystical landscapes of Meghalaya, Assam, and Sikkim. Visit living root bridges, enjoy tranquil lakes, local cuisine, and panoramic views of the Eastern Himalayas.

Wildlife Safari Experience

Duration: 3 Days

Price: ₹15,000 per person

Perfect for nature lovers and photography enthusiasts. Includes guided safaris in Ranthambore or Bandipur National Park with expert naturalists, jungle stay, and wildlife interaction activities.
